RATATOUILLE

Duration: 1h51m | 2007 | G | IMBd rating: 8.1/10

A rat who can cook makes an unusual alliance with a young kitchen worker at a famous Paris restaurant.

Ratatouille is a 2007 animated family comedy-drama about Remy, a rat who dreams of becoming a great chef in Paris. He forms an alliance with a clumsy young kitchen worker, Alfredo Linguini, who is a terrible cook. By secretly hiding under Linguini's hat and pulling his hair, Remy guides Linguini to cook amazing food, leading to success at the famous restaurant of the late chef Auguste Gusteau. Their partnership is challenged by the restaurant's new manager, the harsh food critic Anton Ego, and Remy's own family.
